# BOOK MY SHOW

## FUNCTIONAL
- Book tickets based on
    - current city
    - movie playing
    - nearby theatre
    - available show and seats

## NON FUNCTIONAL
- High concurrency
- High availability
- High consistency
- Secure


## APIs
- Get city, theatreByCity, movieByTheatre, showByMovie, seatsByMovie
- Book seats
- Send tickets by mail
- post reviews and comments
